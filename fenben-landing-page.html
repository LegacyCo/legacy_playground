The Founding Story

Where the
Movement Began

In 2017, Caroline Williams identified a critical gap that no one else was addressing. Researchers and investigators exploring fenbendazole for non-veterinary study had nowhere reliable to turn. The only option available was veterinary-grade paste — poorly standardized, inconsistently dosed, and lacking the quality control required for rigorous investigation.

Caroline identified three core problems: sourcing research-grade fenbendazole from animal formulations was imprecise and unreliable; the lack of standardization made consistent investigation difficult; and there was no accountable, transparent supplier willing to serve the research community’s actual needs.

So she built what didn’t exist. She introduced the first fenbendazole product formulated specifically for non-veterinary research use — and trademarked the name Fenben®. Not to follow a trend. To create the path before a path existed.

Today, The Happy Healing Store is honored to carry that mission forward as the home of the original Fenben® brand — the brand that defined the standard before there was a standard to define.
