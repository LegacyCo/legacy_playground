**Creative Strategy, Scripts & Copy Library**

---

## PLATFORM CONTEXT & STRATEGY

TikTok requires a fundamentally different approach than Google or Meta. The platform rewards authenticity, entertainment, and native content — not traditional advertising. Brand Discipleship's TikTok strategy follows the **"Edu-tainment" model**: deliver real value, build authority, and create organic-feeling content that converts because it earns attention first.

**Core TikTok principle:** Don't make ads. Make content that happens to convert.

**Primary objectives:**
1. Build brand awareness among founders, marketers, and entrepreneurs
2. Drive traffic to lead magnet (Brand Audit Checklist)
3. Generate strategy call bookings from engaged followers/viewers
4. Build retargetable video audience for lower-funnel Meta/YouTube follow-up

#### Script T1 — "3 Seconds" Hook Test (15 sec, Ultra-Short)
**Category:** Awareness / Thumb-Stop Test

```
[ON CAMERA — Founder/Spokesperson, direct to camera, casual setting]

HOOK (0-3s): "If your brand disappeared tomorrow... would anyone notice?"

[Beat. Let it land.]

BODY (3-12s): "Most businesses? Nobody would. Not because they're bad — because their brand doesn't stand for anything.

Brand Discipleship fixes that. We turn customers into disciples."

CTA (12-15s): [Text overlay] "Free Brand Audit Checklist → link in bio"

[Soft Brand Discipleship logo end card]
```

**Visual Notes:**
- Shot on iPhone, natural light — avoid overly produced look
- Speaker looks directly into camera, no script reading
- Fast jump cuts, captions burned in
- Background: clean office or home office, not distracting

#### Script T2 — "5 Signs" (45 sec, Education Format)
**Category:** Awareness / Value-Led

```
[ON CAMERA — energetic, rapid delivery]

HOOK (0-4s): "Five signs your brand is invisible — and costing you money. Number one will hurt."

[Quick cut + text animation for each point]

POINT 1 (4-11s): "You compete on price. Every time. If you're losing deals because you're 'too expensive,' your brand isn't doing its job. Brands with conviction command premium. Period."

POINT 2 (11-18s): "Nobody refers you. Not because your work is bad — but because your brand isn't creating disciples. Customers refer you when they believe in you. Do they?"

POINT 3 (18-25s): "Your team can't explain what you do in one sentence. Brand clarity starts inside. If your people can't articulate your differentiation, your customers definitely can't."

POINT 4 (25-32s): "Your content feels like a chore. Clear brands never run out of content. If every post feels forced, your brand foundation is broken."

POINT 5 (32-39s): "You look just like your competition. Same colors, same language, same offers. Invisible."

CTA (39-45s): "If any of these hit — download our free Brand Audit Checklist. Link in bio. 27 questions, 20 minutes, real clarity."

[Text overlay: "Free Brand Audit → Link in Bio"]
```

#### Script T5 — "POV Brand Teardown" (30–45 sec)
**Category:** Entertainment / Virality / Education

```
[Text on screen: "POV: You asked me to review your brand 👀"]
[ON CAMERA — reviewer energy, scrolling/reviewing setup]

HOOK (0-4s): "Okay I'm looking at your website and I have some thoughts..."

BODY (4-35s): [As if reviewing a real but anonymized brand]

"First — love the photography. Genuinely great. But...

Your headline says 'We deliver results-driven solutions for your business needs.' That's... every business. That's nobody.

Tell me what you specifically do, who specifically you help, and why specifically they should pick you over anyone else.

And this color palette? Same navy and white as literally 60% of B2B brands. You're invisible in a sea of blue.

Here's what I'd change: [gives 2-3 quick, actionable brand fixes]

This isn't an attack — this is what Brand Discipleship calls The Brand Leak. And almost every brand has it."

CTA (35-43s): "Want your own teardown? Drop your website in the comments. Or grab the free Brand Audit Checklist in bio and do it yourself."
```

Download the free Brand Audit Checklist if you want to build these out. Link in bio."

## A/B TESTING PLAN — TIKTOK

| Variable | Test A | Test B | KPI |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hook style | Question hook | Shocking statement hook | 3-sec view rate |
| Presenter | Founder (personal story) | Expert authority (talking head) | Watch-through rate |
| Length | Short (15–20 sec) | Long (45–60 sec) | Completion rate + CTR |
| Caption | Long-form educational | Short + punchy | CTR from caption link |
| CTA | Lead magnet (download) | Direct to strategy call | CPL + lead quality |
| Content type | Pure education (tips) | Brand teardown (entertainment) | Shares + saves |
| Production style | iPhone/raw | Slightly produced (but not polished) | Authenticity perception |

## CREATOR BRIEF TEMPLATE (For Influencer/UGC Partnerships)

**Brand:** Brand Discipleship (branddiscipleship.com)
**Product:** Brand strategy services + Free Brand Audit Checklist lead magnet
**Tone:** Authentic, educated, founder-friendly. NOT salesy or corporate.

**Messaging Do's:**
- Speak from personal experience ("I used to struggle with...")
- Frame Brand Discipleship as a tool/resource, not a pitch
- Focus on the free value first (Brand Audit Checklist), not the paid services
- Use your own words — we want your voice, not ours

**Messaging Don'ts:**
- Don't make specific income/revenue claims without verification
- Don't use corporate/formal language
- Don't script it perfectly — raw and real wins on TikTok
- Don't oversell — educate, then offer

**Required elements:**
- Mention "Brand Discipleship" by name at least once
- Include "link in bio" CTA directing to Brand Audit Checklist or strategy call
- Use at least 3 of the approved hashtags in caption

**Deliverables:** 1x primary video (30–60 sec) + 1x TikTok Story (optional)
**Approval process:** Submit script/outline for review before filming. Final video requires approval before posting.
