## UNIFIED MESSAGING FRAMEWORK — QUICK REFERENCE

### The Brand Promise
> "We turn brands into movements."

### The Three Beliefs (Value Pillars)
1. **Belief > Awareness** — People remember brands they believe in, not just ones they've seen.
2. **System > Campaign** — Systems create sustainable growth. Campaigns create temporary traffic.
3. **Disciple > Customer** — A disciple brings you 10 customers. A customer brings you zero.

### The Brand Voice in One Line
> Bold, direct, and inspiring — like a visionary mentor who cuts through the noise.

### The CTA Hierarchy
| Stage | CTA |
|---|---|
| Awareness | Watch free training / Read the framework |
| Consideration | Download Brand Audit Checklist |
| Decision | Book Free Brand Strategy Call |
| Retention | Join Brand Discipleship Community |

## A/B TESTING PRIORITY QUEUE

**High priority (run immediately):**
1. Homepage hero headline framing (Problem-led vs. Aspiration-led)
2. Primary CTA copy ("Book Free Call" vs. "Get Brand Clarity")
3. Google Ads: Problem hook vs. Outcome hook
4. Meta video: Question hook vs. Statement hook
5. Email subject lines: Question vs. Provocative statement

**Medium priority (run after baseline established):**
6. Landing page length (full scroll vs. concise hero + CTA)
7. Lead magnet vs. direct call booking as primary offer
8. Email welcome sequence length (5 emails vs. 7 emails)
9. Social: Long-form vs. short-form content performance
10. Proposal: 2 options vs. 3 tiered options
