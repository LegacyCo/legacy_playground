## CAMPAIGN ARCHITECTURE

```
Account
├── Awareness Campaigns (TOF)
│   ├── Video: "The Invisible Brand" — Cold Audience
│   ├── Video: "Customer vs. Disciple" — Lookalike 1%
│   └── Static: Brand Education — Interest Targeting
├── Consideration Campaigns (MOF)
│   ├── Lead Gen: Brand Audit Download — Website Visitors
│   ├── Engagement: Free Webinar/Training — Engaged Audience
│   └── Traffic: Framework Page — Warm Retarget
└── Conversion Campaigns (BOF)
    ├── Conversion: Book Strategy Call — Retarget Engagers
    ├── Conversion: Apply Now — Retarget Email Openers
    └── Retargeting: Cart/Form Abandoners — Urgency Messaging
```

## AUDIENCE STRATEGY

### Cold Audiences (TOF)
- **Interest-based:** Entrepreneurship, Business Strategy, Brand Management, Marketing, Seth Godin, Simon Sinek, Gary Vaynerchuk, Donald Miller (StoryBrand), Business owners
- **Demographic:** Business owners, C-suite titles, Marketing Directors, 28–55, HHI $75K+
- **Behavior:** Small business owners, engaged shoppers (B2B behaviors)

### Warm Audiences (MOF)
- Website visitors (30/60/90 days)
- Video viewers (25%, 50%, 75% watch-through)
- Instagram/Facebook page engagers (60/90 days)
- Lead magnet downloaders

### Hot Audiences (BOF)
- Website visitors (7/14 days) who visited key pages (pricing, apply, strategy)
- Email subscribers (custom audience upload)
- Form starters who didn't complete
- Lookalike 1% of buyers/best leads

## AD COPY A/B TESTING MATRIX

| Element | Variant A | Variant B | Metric |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hook style | Question ("Is your brand invisible?") | Provocative statement ("Your brand is costing you money") | Thumb-stop rate (3-sec views) |
| Body copy length | Short (50–80 words) | Long-form (200+ words) | CTR + CVR by placement |
| Offer | Free consultation | Free download | Lead volume + quality |
| Social proof | Testimonial quote | Result metric ("40% price increase") | CVR |
| Video format | Talking head (founder) | Text/motion graphics only | View rate + CTR |
| CTA language | "Book Free Call" | "Claim Your Spot" | CTR |
| Audience | Broad interest | 1% lookalike | CPA |
| Creative style | Bold text/minimal | Lifestyle/aspirational imagery | CTR + ROAS |
